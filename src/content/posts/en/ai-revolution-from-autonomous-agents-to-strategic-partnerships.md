## Core idea

The migrated article argues that the next AI shift is not just better answers. It is the movement from isolated tools toward agents that can coordinate work, preserve context, and support strategic execution.

The durable lesson is organizational: autonomy needs boundaries. A useful agent must know the task, the evidence, the approval path, and the moment when it should stop and ask for review.
