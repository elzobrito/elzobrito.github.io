## Core idea

The original post argues that asking a model to "find bugs" is too loose for serious security work. ESAA-Security reframes the job as a governed process with scope, evidence, boundaries, review, and reproducible findings.

The important distinction is between intuition and auditability. A useful security agent should leave a trail that another reviewer can inspect.
